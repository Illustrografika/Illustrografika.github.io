WELCOME TO ILLUSTROGRAFIKA
Illustrografika is an independent duo of game developers from Eastern Europe. We make games that intertwine philosophy, culture, storytelling and art. Illustrografika is about opening doors to other times and other places, to the terrible and
beautiful tales being told just beyond the threshold.

02. PRINCIPLES
ROOTSPeople are borne by the tides onto the warm shores of their homeland. Cultural memories and perspectives will inevitably seep into every crevice of an idea. In the face of that, one must take heed to depict the world in it’s diverse infinity.
CRAFTWork, more as craft and less than production. Every element, no matter how small, is painstaking and intentional, helps to form a harmonious structure where every action, detail and emotion matters.
POISEToday, the world ends. Gentle moonlight illuminates a serpent opening it’s jaw, a man who has no mercy for another, a dog lashed one too many times. Don’t worry. Tomorrow, pick up the pieces and try again.
